Union Budget 2026–27: New Income Tax Rates Explained – Who Gains & Who Loses?

Feb 04, 2026 | Bhatt Consultancy Services | Tax Rate Analysis

The Union Budget 2026–27 introduces revised tax slabs that shift effective take-home pay across income bands. Here is a clear, decision-ready view of who benefits most.

What Changed in the Slabs

  • Updated slab thresholds aimed at middle-income relief.
  • Lower marginal rates for specific mid-tier bands.
  • Simplified rebate rules for entry-level taxpayers.

Who Gains the Most

Mid-income professionals and early-stage founders see the highest effective rate reduction when combining the new slabs with standard deductions.
